About Muni | HCL Hackathon

Muni is an AI-powered diary and self-care app where you can share your thoughts and feelings! Let Muni help you out by providing self-care tips once it detects your emotions from your diary entries!

You need to sign up for full access of Muni! Fortunately, you can try out a limited version of the app by clicking the “Try Demo” button below.
